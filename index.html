Best Desserts in Little Italy!

Simply Biscotti is not just a Café… It is a labour of love for founder Rosa Pino. Over two decades, Rosa had perfected her recipes for her customers, and every day you’ll be sure to find something delicious, wholesome and always freshly made in her kitchen. That is her promise to you.

Menu

Lunch

We take pride in making your meal using only the highest quality, freshest ingredients so that you receive the most delicious, home made meal every time, just like we make for our family! From mouth-watering paninis and frittatas to succulent salads and soups, our menu will be sure to surprise your senses and delight your taste buds.

Desserts

Over the past 20 years, Rosa and her team have been waking up at the crack of dawn in order to make sure everything in the pastry case is fresh, delicious, and made just for you! From twice-baked biscotti and salty-sweet-crunchy-soft ‘Lobster tails’ to gorgeous cheesecakes, tarts and pies, Simply Biscotti has the perfect dessert to put a smile on your face.

Catering

Simply Biscotti has always been sought out for its delicious creations inside the cafes, but did you know you can also experience their fresh, hand-made fare at your next office meeting or special occasion? Simply Biscotti’s catering team will listen to your needs and suggest various options for your next event so that you can focus on all the other details.

Please call 613.234.5336 and let us help you make your next event a huge success by leaving the food to us. It’s what we do best!
